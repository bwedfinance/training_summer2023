Data Sources

The EdBuild data for Kentucky was pulled from the edbuildr package on 2022-07-19.

Map data for Kentucky districts was pulled from the edbuilmapr package on 2022-07-19.

More detail on both packages can be found on EdBuild’s website.

Mapping KY data

Poverty

The highest poverty rates are found in Kentucky’s southeastern districts. All of these districts are in the Appalachian region.

Urbanicity

The NCES urbanicity definitions are pretty granular.

If we simplifiy to four urbanicity categories, the high frequency of rural districts becomes more apparent.

Local Revenue

There’s a clear outlier in local revenue per pupil: Anchorage Independent.

Urbanicity and local revenue

Rural districts generate significantly less revenue than urban or suburban districts in Kentucky.
